What is the Best Neighborhood in Pittsburgh?

An in-class group project that used data analysis to determine which Pittsburgh neighborhood is best for athletes. I focused on baseball as my metric, analyzing the number of fields and lighting availability across neighborhoods. Using Pandas and Matplotlib in JupyterLab, I created visualizations to compare neighborhoods and highlight the most athletic-friendly areas.
